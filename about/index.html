About us

It's not rocket science.

We are aerospace engineers from the Space Coast. We spent years building systems where a missed checklist item or an untested line of code was not an option. Then we looked around Brevard County and saw something that bothered us.

The businesses we rely on, the HVAC company in Palm Bay, the dental office in Viera, the marine repair shop near Port Canaveral, were drowning. Not in customers. In manual work. Quotes typed twice. Invoices tracked on paper and in three apps that do not talk to each other. Job data copied from a text message into a spreadsheet into accounting software by hand. Owners working until 9 p.m. doing data entry that software should have handled hours ago.

The same rigor that puts hardware into orbit can fix this. So we started Space Coast Automation to bring it to the businesses next door.

Our mission

Software that actually works, owned by you

Give Main Street businesses on the Space Coast the kind of software that fits how you operate, is owned by you, and is maintained by people you can reach. We measure success in hours given back to owners and staff, quotes that go out the same day, and the end of double entry.

What we value
  • Outcomes over features. We start with the result you need, then build only what gets you there.
  • Plain talk. No jargon walls. We tell you what we are building, what it costs, and what changes for you.
  • You own it. The software runs your business. We document everything and hand you the keys.
  • Reliability is the product. Software that breaks on a busy Friday is worse than no software. We test before you trust.
  • Local and reachable. We are down the road, not in a call center three time zones away.

Let's talk

Tell us where your day gets stuck. We will tell you, in plain terms, what we would build, what it costs to start, and what changes for you. No jargon, no pressure.
