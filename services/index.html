01

Business Process & Workflow Automation

Who it is for

Owners and office managers doing the same thing by hand every day. If someone on your team copies data between a spreadsheet, an email, and an invoicing tool, or spends Friday afternoon assembling a report from screenshots, this is for you.

What we do

We map the steps you take now, find the ones a computer should own, and connect your existing tools so the work happens on its own. We do not rip out what works. We bridge the gaps between the software you already pay for. Where it earns its place, we add AI: software that reads incoming emails and sorts them, drafts a first-pass quote from a photo and a few notes, or pulls the key numbers off a scanned delivery ticket. You review the output. It does the typing.

Example deliverables and use cases

  • A quote that builds itself: a customer fills out a form, the system pulls your pricing, and a draft estimate lands in your inbox to approve and send. Same day, not next week.
  • Order intake that stops the double entry: an email order flows straight into your job board and your accounting tool, once.
  • A Monday morning report that writes itself: yesterday's sales, open jobs, and overdue invoices in one email, no spreadsheet wrangling.
  • AI inbox triage for a Melbourne clinic: new patient messages sorted into urgent, scheduling, and billing before staff open them.
  • Permit and inspection tracking for a contractor: deadlines logged, reminders sent, nothing missed with the county.

The outcome: Hours back every week. Fewer errors from manual entry. Work that used to wait on one person now runs whether they are in or not.

02

Website Design & Development

Who it is for

Businesses whose website is out of date, hard to update, or missing entirely. If your customers find you on their phone and the site looks broken, or if you cannot change your own hours without calling a developer, this is for you.

What we do

We design and build sites that load fast, read clearly on a phone, and turn visitors into calls and bookings. Clean structure, honest copy, and the contact path front and center. We build it so you can update text, hours, and photos yourself. We handle the parts that decide whether you get found: page speed, mobile layout, and local search setup so a search for your trade in Rockledge or Titusville actually surfaces you.

Example deliverables and use cases

  • A new site for a Cocoa Beach surf shop: product highlights, store hours, and a map, fast on mobile for tourists searching from the sand.
  • A booking-focused site for a Satellite Beach salon: services, prices, and a book-now button that fills the calendar.
  • A trade site for a Palm Bay HVAC company: service areas, a quote request form, and a click-to-call button on every page.
  • A redesign that fixes a slow, cluttered site so it loads in under two seconds and reads well on any screen.
  • Local search setup so you show up when someone nearby searches your service.

The outcome: More calls and bookings from the same traffic. A site you control. A first impression that matches the quality of your work.

03

Web & Mobile App Development

Who it is for

Businesses that have outgrown spreadsheets and off-the-shelf tools. If you are running operations through a tangle of shared sheets, or you need something specific that no product on the market does, you need software built for the job.

What we do

We build custom web and mobile apps: the tool your team opens every day to run the work. A field app your crews use on site. A dashboard you check from your phone. A customer portal where clients see their job status without calling you. We build only what you need, in stages, so you see working software early and pay for value as it lands, not a year of invoices before anything runs.

Example deliverables and use cases

  • A field service app for a Brevard plumbing company: techs log jobs, capture photos, and collect signatures on a phone, synced back to the office in real time.
  • An inventory app for a Merritt Island marine supplier: stock counts, reorder alerts, and supplier orders in one place.
  • A customer portal for a Viera property manager: tenants submit maintenance requests and track status, cutting phone tag.
  • A scheduling app for a multi-location service business: jobs, crews, and routes coordinated across Melbourne, Palm Bay, and West Melbourne.
  • An internal dashboard that pulls your numbers into one screen you can read in ten seconds.

The outcome: Software that fits how you actually work, not the other way around. Less time fighting tools. A system that scales as you grow.

04

Systems Integration & Consulting

Who it is for

Businesses with software that does not talk to each other, or owners weighing a software decision who want a straight answer before they spend. If your point-of-sale, your accounting, and your scheduling all live in separate worlds, this is for you.

What we do

We connect the systems you already run so data moves between them without a person retyping it. Point-of-sale to accounting. Scheduling to invoicing. Online store to inventory. One source of truth instead of four versions of it. We also advise. Bring us the question and we give you a plain answer: whether to buy or build, which tool fits, what a project will really take, including when the answer is to do nothing yet.

Example deliverables and use cases

  • Point-of-sale to accounting integration for a Cocoa Village retailer: every sale posts to the books automatically, no month-end reconciliation marathon.
  • Scheduling to invoicing for a service company: a completed job becomes an invoice without anyone opening a second app.
  • An online store connected to in-store inventory so you never sell what you do not have.
  • A technology review for an owner deciding between three vendors: a one-page recommendation with costs and tradeoffs spelled out.
  • A migration plan to move off a tool that is being discontinued, with no data left behind.

The outcome: Systems that act like one system. No more double entry, no more conflicting numbers. Decisions made with a clear picture instead of a sales pitch.
